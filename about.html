About Us

RQS Medical Physics Consulting LLC is a specialized radiation safety and medical physics consulting firm serving hospitals, outpatient clinics, imaging centers, and healthcare systems across Northern California.

We provide expert guidance in radiation protection, regulatory compliance, imaging safety, and clinical medical physics. Our mission is to help healthcare institutions maintain the highest standards of safety, accuracy, and operational excellence in environments where radiation is used for diagnosis or treatment.

Who we are

RQS is led by a board-certified Medical Physicist with extensive experience in health physics, radiation safety program management, and clinical medical physics. We bring deep technical knowledge, regulatory expertise, and a responsive service model tailored to the needs of modern healthcare organizations.

We believe in precision, transparency, and proactive safety, ensuring our clients remain fully compliant while supporting safe, efficient clinical operations.
